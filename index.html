About Us

AllergEat is an initiative dedicated to improving the lives of people with food allergies, intolerances, and dietary restrictions. Our mission is to facilitate the search for restaurants offering safe and delicious options for everyone.

We understand the challenges faced by people with dietary restrictions and strive to create an inclusive community where everyone can enjoy the experience of eating out without worries.
